01 — Display

Your Agent,
at a glance

The 1.3-inch circular OLED sits at the heart of the triangular body. It displays real-time Agent status, task progress, and expressive pixel-art faces — turning cold code execution into a warm, tangible companion.

When your Agent finishes, errors out, or needs confirmation, Power Up doesn't just notify — it physically calls for your attention through haptic vibration and TTS voice output.

02 — Dual Mode

Power bank by day.
AI terminal on demand.

Unconnected, Power Up is a premium 10000mAh portable charger with a folding USB-C plug. The triangular form sits perfectly in your palm or stands upright on any surface.

Connect via BLE 5.3 or USB-C to your laptop or phone, and it transforms into a dedicated AI Agent controller — voice commands flow through JoyInside ASR, responses come back as text, speech, and ambient light.

03 — Seamless Pairing

04 — Feature Design

6 core designs, in one palm.

01

Voice Interaction

Dual MEMS microphone array with noise cancellation. Press the physical MIC button, speak naturally. JoyInside ASR delivers real-time transcription with dialect support.

02

Circular OLED Status

240x240 resolution, SPI-driven, ultra-low power. Displays Agent state, task progress, pixel-art expressions. Your code's heartbeat, visible at a glance.

03

Physical Controls

Metal scroll wheel to switch Agents and modes. Dedicated confirm (green) and cancel (red) buttons. Zero learning curve, pure tactile feedback.

04

BLE 5.3 + USB-C

Wireless Bluetooth for Vibe Coding mobility. Wired USB-C for low-latency data + PD charging passthrough. VBUS detection auto-switches modes seamlessly.

05

Folding USB-C Charger

Integrated folding USB-C male connector on the rear. Flip open to charge any device. Folds completely flush when stored — form meets function.

06

JoyInside AI Stack

Five AI capabilities: Context-Aware ASR, Live TTS with emotion, Emotional Dialogue, Memory Layering, and Gamification Engine. Full-stack intelligence.

05 — Structure

Seven precision layers

Every millimeter engineered. Aluminum alloy shell, custom triangular LiPo battery, ESP32-S3 brain, dual MEMS microphones — compressed into a palm-sized triangle.
